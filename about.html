why choose us?

This weather website was designed for the benefit of the citizens. As a backup to the national weather, the JARS website seeks to provide up-to-date, accurate, and thorough weather updates for areas in the Philippines. It can also provide precise local weather forecasts for every location on land in the Philippines, as well as the most accurate forecasts for the coming days.
